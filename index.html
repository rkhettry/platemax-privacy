PlateMax Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

PlateMax is a nutrition and workout tracking app. We take your privacy seriously and collect only the minimum data needed to provide the service.

Data We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier. If you choose to share your email, we store that as well. We do not receive or store your Apple ID password.

Nutrition Data: Meal descriptions, nutritional information, water intake, and dietary goals you enter into the app.

Workout Data: Exercise names, sets, reps, weights, and workout notes you enter into the app.

Photos: If you use the camera feature to log meals, photos are sent to our server for analysis and are not stored permanently.

How We Use Your Data

Your nutrition and workout data is used solely to provide the tracking and insights features of the app. Meal descriptions and photos may be processed by OpenAI's API to extract nutritional information. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). Data is also cached locally on your device for offline access. All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. Upon account deletion, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase - Authentication and data storage
  • OpenAI - Meal and workout text analysis
  • Apple Sign-In - Authentication

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
